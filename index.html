FitFind Mirror

FitFind Mirror is an interactive mirror complete with the day’s calendar and weather information, music playing capabilities, and clothing identification AI designed to help you get ready for the day, choose the right attire for the weather, and leave the house on time.

All within the mirror you already use to get ready in the morning, you now have a fully integrated way to view all the date, time, and weather information you need to plan your day and even select the right outfit with the help of an AI-assisted camera. Before you start getting ready for the day, you can turn on your favorite Spotify playlist and play it through any of your external speakers. FitFind Mirror seamlessly blends into your space, invisibly integrated behind the glass to maintain your mirror’s design while subtly complimenting it with helpful assistive capabilities.

Motivation

Motivation

Though a mirror has long been an integral support to one’s morning routine and new reflectivity techniques have enabled new use cases utilizing one- and two-way mirrors, the growing popularity of the smart home, with its internet-connected assistants, appliances, and gadgets, seems to have skipped the centerpiece hanging atop the vanity.

Without changing the way you get ready in the morning or interrupting your relationship with your reflection, your mirror serves as a blank slate for enhancing your morning routine. By displaying everything you need to know for the day right next to you, you can more quickly instantly identify the day without taking your eyes off your half-shaved face or half-applied eyeliner.

If you’re someone who loves to listen to music in the shower, why not ask your mirror to play your Spotify playlist as you hop in the shower? If you happened to need to leave the house in a hurry, you might forget to to grab the hat or outerwear you wanted to have for the snow that’s forecasted to fall in the afternoon. Using a camera integrated into the glass, your mirror can give you a quick reminder to grab the right attire before you run out the door.

By seamlessly integrating more convenient ways for you to perform the prerequisite tasks you repeatedly do to get ready in the morning, you can now be more confident that your attire will keep you comfortable in the day’s weather, you will leave your house on time, and your Spotify Wrapped will include all the songs you duetted in the shower.
